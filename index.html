Some Word About the School

Tricent is a worldclass medical School based in Nairobi,Kenya, that aims to equip students with skills and knowledge to help them become experienced specialists in the medical field

As a school, we are bent on, not only to produce experienced professionals, but also to furnish our students with the core life values that will make them ethical and valuable citizens.
