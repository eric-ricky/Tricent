TSMHST MISSION STATEMENT

Mission

Provide humane and integral health sciences education, business and technology with emphasis on primary health care indepressed and underserved communities especially in rural areas, and to keep pace with developing global technology.

Vision

To be the leading training institution that envisions healthy, business and technology in communities with quality education.

Motto

Celebrating professional excellence
